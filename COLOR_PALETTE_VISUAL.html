Color Usage Guide

🤝 Offer Activities
Use Teal (#0D9488) for buttons, badges, and markers showing services being offered.
🙌 Need Activities
Use Rose (#F87171) for buttons, badges, and markers for requests/needs.
✨ Secondary Actions
Use Amber (#F59E0B) for highlights, secondary buttons, and category badges.
✅ Contact Button
Use WhatsApp Green (#25D366) for the primary contact/messaging action.

Accessibility Information

Why These Colors?

For Young People (18-35): Teal feels modern and fresh. Amber is energetic and vibrant. Rose is contemporary and approachable.

For Adults (35-65): All colors are warm and professional. High contrast ensures readability. Clean design feels trustworthy.

For Seniors (65+): Warm color palette is less harsh on aging eyes. Large buttons (44px+) are easy to tap. Clear typography ensures readability. High contrast ratios (4.5:1) meet accessibility standards.
